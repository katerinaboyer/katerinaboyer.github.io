About Me

Howdy!

My name is Katerina, and I'm a Computer Scientist & Engineer from Allen, TX (byline work in progress). I'm currenty a senior at Texas A&M University pursuing my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science & Engineering, with minors in Business and Cybersecurity. The former is sort of an accident (so don't hold me to it), but I am particularly interested in the law and policies surrounding our online world, among other fields.

Outside of school and work, some of my more ~cliche~ passions are travelling, cooking and baking, and training my dog new tricks. Additionally, I am particularly keen on etiology and epidemiology, before COVID was relevant. One of my favorite books as a kid was "Fever 1793" about yellow fever in Philadelphia and ever since I've been devouring books about disease outbreaks. Additionally, I maintain an ever expanding garden mainly filled with golden pothos clones, collect niche perfumes, and binge watch NPR Tiny Desk Concert videos on YouTube; I recommend Sudan Archives to everyone!!

Inside of school and work, most of my experience is in web and mobile development, full stack. You can check out my Projects page for a more in depth look, or see my resume above. In the field of software engineering, I think I would like to stay in web development as I think it provides an array of ever emerging opportunities. Further, I have interests in low code development environments to make software engineering more accessible to those without technical backgrounds, as well as search engine optimization, and data science. I think the next career frontier for me would be getting experience with data visualization and analytics.

To sum it up, I like to try new things to keep myself on my toes and I'm always looking for new and interesting opportunities to stretch and grow with!
